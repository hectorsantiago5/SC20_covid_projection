Covid-19 is spread by people that are in close contact with one another or when an infected person is coughing, sneezing, talking, or simply breathing.

# Symptoms How to Protect Yourself
1 Fever and/or Vomiting Wash your hands after every public encounter
2 Cough and/or Sore Throat Use hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol
3 Difficulty breathing When around others, keep at least a six feet distance from one another
4 Loss of taste and/or smell When around others, wear a mask that covers your mouth and nose

How does Covid-19 Spread?

Covid-19 is spread by people that are in close contact with one another or when an infected person is coughing, sneezing, talking, or simply breathing.

Who is most at risk for Covid-19?

Adults of older age, anyone with underlying health conditions, and pregnant women are most at risk with Covid-19.

Symptoms
Fever and/or Vomiting
Cough and/or Sore Throat
Difficulty breathing
Loss of taste and/or smell
How to Protect Yourself
Wash your hands after every public encounter
Use hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol
When around others, keep at least a six feet distance from one another
When around others, wear a mask that covers your mouth and nose
